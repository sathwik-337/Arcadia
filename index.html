  • Squad Mode: 4 players per team
  • Perspective: TPP only (Classic Erangel)

  • Stream sniping = disqualification
  • Team name and ID must match registration

🎮 Valorant Rules

  • Team size: 5 players per team
  • Best of 1 format (Finals BO3)
  • Map veto by team captains
  • Mic and headphones mandatory
  • Cheating = disqualification
